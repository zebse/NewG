    About Us

    Since its establishment NGUC had accomplished several successful achievements, including launching three campuses in: Addis Ababa, capital city of Ethiopia, in Nekemte, East Wollega and a campus served for 6 years in Mettu, Ilu Aba Bora Zone. Moreover, the institution also currently runs a campus in, Harggeisa, Somaliland in a cross border modality.

    Mission

    The mission of NGUC is providing quality higher education to produce competent graduates, conducting need-based researches that alleviate societal problems, and offering need based multidimensional community services to the community through a superior and interdisciplinary academic program; training the new generation of undergraduates and graduate level studies in desired professional skills.

    Vision

    To become a center of excellence with commitment to a holistic learning and teaching environment that produces qualified graduates who have as a result developed a sincere spirit of tolerance and a sense of commitment to society, by 2023.
