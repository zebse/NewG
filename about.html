    About Us

    Since its establishment NGUC had accomplished several successful achievements, including launching three campuses in: Addis Ababa, capital city of Ethiopia, in Nekemte, East Wollega and a campus served for 6 years in Mettu, Ilu Aba Bora Zone. Moreover, the institution also currently runs a campus in, Harggeisa, Somaliland in a cross border modality.

    Mission

    The mission of NGUC is providing quality higher education to produce competent graduates, conducting need-based researches that alleviate societal problems, and offering need based multidimensional community services to the community through a superior and interdisciplinary academic program; training the new generation of undergraduates and graduate level studies in desired professional skills.

    Vision

    To become a center of excellence with commitment to a holistic learning and teaching environment that produces qualified graduates who have as a result developed a sincere spirit of tolerance and a sense of commitment to society, by 2023.

    Core values

    Core Values are the fundamental values or ideals at the heart of the organization. The core values of NGUC are:

    • Effective management delivers development benefits to Ethiopia.
    • New technologies and knowledge overcome development barriers.
    • Shared knowledge solves community problems together.
    • Continuous learning and Innovativeness.
    • Effective Community Based Quality Service.
    • Maximum utilization of capacity and resources.

    Organizational Structure Description

    Our organization is structured to ensure effective governance, academic excellence, and operational efficiency. At the top level, the Board serves as the highest governing body, providing overall strategic direction and oversight. The Board is supported directly by three key roles:

    • Presidnet
    • Legal Advisor
    • External Advisor

    President’s Office

    The President leads the university’s executive functions and reports directly to the Board. Reporting to the President are:

    • External Auditor:Ensures independent financial and operational audits for transparency and accountability.
    • Academic Senate:Provides academic policy guidance and governance input.
    • Vice President:Oversees the day-to-day academic and administrative operations of the university.

    Vice President’s Portfolio

    The Vice President manages the following major divisions:

    • Quality Assurance:Responsible for maintaining academic standards, accreditation, and continuous improvement initiatives.
    • Academic Dean: Leads the university’s academic programs. It manages Departments dean, Library, Program coordinator, Registrar, Post graduate, Student service and Research
    • Administrative and Finance Division: Responsible for overall operational support and financial. Includes: HRM(Human Resource Management), Finance and Budget and Internal Audit

    Additionally, the Legal Advisor and External Advisor provide specialized guidance to the Board and President, ensuring legal compliance and strategic alignment with external stakeholders.
